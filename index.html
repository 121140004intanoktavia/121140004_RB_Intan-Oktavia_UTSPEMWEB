About ITERA's Library Membership Site

As a tech institute that always working on development, ITERA wont missed out over technology around us. To make administration more easier and efficient, ITERA decide to do a digitalisation for campus literacy. This site serve you a membership apply feature. By clicking the form site on the side bar, you'll find the instruction to fill the form with your data. The data will be proceed to be your e-library account. With e-library account, you'll have a free access for all ITERA's books, references, journals, and more, everytime and everywhere! Join the membership now!
